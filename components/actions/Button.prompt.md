Pill-shaped action button; primary CTAs get a one-pass silver shine sweep on hover.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary" size="lg" iconRight={<Icon name="arrow-right" size={16} />}>
  Read the guide
</Button>
<Button variant="secondary">Browse the library</Button>
```

- `primary` deep forest green — one per view.
- `secondary` warm white with silver hairline border.
- `chrome` metallic gradient — reserve for premium/paid moments (ebook purchase).
- `ghost` text-only; `dark` for green/charcoal surfaces.
- Sentence-case labels, calm verbs ("Read the guide", never "BUY NOW").
